Arise

Build better habits, one day at a time

Arise runs on your phone through a free app called Expo Go. Follow the two steps below to get set up.

STEP 1

Download Expo Go

Choose the button for your phone's operating system.

iPhone — App Store Android — Google Play
STEP 2

Scan the QR code from the server

Once Expo Go is installed, ask the person running the server to show you the QR code in their terminal window. Then:

iPhone: Open your Camera app, point it at the QR code, and tap the banner that appears at the top of your screen.

Android: Open Expo Go, tap "Scan QR code", and point it at the QR code.

Your phone and the server computer must be on the same Wi-Fi network.
